                                        <p>Ingredients:</p>
                                        <ul type="1">
                                        <li>¼ cup flour, 1 egg</li>
                                        <li>½ cup panko breadcrumbs ,¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese</li>
                                        <li>1KG chicken cutlets, trimmed</li>
                                        <li>Salt and pepper to taste, 1 clove garlic</li>
                                        <li>1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar</li>
                                        <li>2 cups cherry tomatoes, cut into eighths</li>

                            <ul type="1">
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 1: </span>Spread flour in a shallow dish or pie pan. Whisk egg and water in another shallow dish. Combine panko and Parmesan in a third dish.</li>

                        <div class="modal-content">
                            <h2>Chickpea Burgers</h2>
                            <div class="container text-center">
                                <div class="row">
                                    <div class="col">
                                        <p>Ingredients:</p>
                                        <ul type="1">
                                        <li>2 tsp sesame seeds, 1 tsp ground coriander</li>
                                        <li>4 scallions, chopped, 3 cloves garlic</li>
                                        <li>250gm chickpeas Rinsed</li>
                                        <li>1 cup cooked brown rice</li>
                                        <li>3 tablespoons lemon juice</li>
                                        <li>4 6-inch whole-wheat pita breads, warmed</li>
                                        <li>4 tablespoons nonfat plain yogurt</li>
                                        </ul>
                                    </div>  
                                    <div class="col colimg">
                                        <img src="images/ChickpeaBurgers.jpg" class="modal-image" alt="Modal Image">
                                        <br>
                                    </div>
                                </div>
                            </div>
                            <p>Instructions:</p>
                            <ul type="1">
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 1: </span> Heat a small skillet over low heat. Add sesame seeds and toast, stirring, until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes. Add coriander and cumin; cook, stirring, until fragrant, 10 to 20 seconds more. Transfer to a plate and let cool. </li>
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 2: </span> Add oil to the pan and heat over medium heat. Add scallions and garlic; cook, stirring, until softened, about 2 minutes. Set aside. </li>
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 3: </span> Position rack in upper third of oven; preheat broiler. (Alternatively, preheat a grill to medium-high.)</li>
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 4: </span>Coarsely mash chickpeas in a medium bowl with a potato masher. Stir in rice, 1/3 cup wheat germ, lemon juice, salt, pepper, the reserved spice mixture and the scallion mixture; mix well. Shape the mixture into four 3/4-inch-thick patties. Place the remaining 1/3 cup wheat germ in a shallow dish and dredge the patties, pressing the wheat germ onto them firmly. If broiling, place the patties on a lightly oiled rack set on a baking sheet.</li>
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 5: </span>Grill the patties until browned and heated through, about 3 minutes per side. Tuck the burgers into pitas with tomato slices, spinach and yogurt. </li>
                            </ul>

                                        <li>⅔ cup water, ½ cup whole-wheat couscous</li>

                                        <li>½ cup whole-wheat couscous</li>
                                        <li>¼ cup pitted Kalamata olives</li>
                                        <li>¼ cup crumbled feta cheese</li>

                            <ul type="1">
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 1: </span> Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan. Stir in couscous, cover and remove from heat. Allow to stand until the liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork; set aside. </li>
                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 2: </span> Prepare Green Beans, Tahini Sauce. Divide among 4 small condiment containers with lids and refrigerate.</li>

                            <li><span style="font-weight: bold;">Step 4: </span> To serve, reheat in the microwave until heated through, about 2 minutes. Dress with tahini sauce just before eating.</li>
